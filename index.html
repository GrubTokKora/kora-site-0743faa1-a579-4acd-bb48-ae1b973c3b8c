About us

The cafe which turns the cravings into satisfaction.

At Pravah, we built a place for everyday cravings—whether you are stopping by for a quick bite, a full plate with friends, or a late-night pick-me-up. Our kitchen brings together familiar comfort and bold flavour, served with the warmth you would expect from a neighbourhood cafe.

From cutting chai, maska bun, and dum chai to hearty Indo-Chinese plates—noodles, fried rice, and snacks in single or full portions—there is something for every appetite. Pair that with our chicken and prawns pakodi, and Pravah specials such as Patthar Ka Gosht and Mutton Marag, and you get a menu that is made to share, celebrate small wins, and satisfy real hunger.

Come for the aroma, stay for the taste—and leave with the feeling that your craving was met, exactly the way you wanted it.

On the menu

  • Chai & maska — Irani dum, zafrani, ginger, and more, with fresh bun and maska options.
  • Indo-Chinese — Snacks, noodles, and fried rice for veg, egg, and chicken lovers.
  • Pakodi & specials — Chicken and prawns by weight, plus Patthar Ka Gosht and house favourites.
